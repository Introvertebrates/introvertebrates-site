<h2>What was found</h2>

<p>

  Researchers described <em>Balticolasma wunderlichi</em>, a fossil harvestman preserved in amber from Ukraine and the Baltic region, dating to about 35 million years ago.

</p>

<p>

  The find is especially notable because it represents the first known fossil member of the subfamily Ortholasmatinae.

</p>



<h2>Why it matters</h2>

<p>

  Although this is broader arachnology rather than spider-specific research, it helps fill in part of the deeper evolutionary history of arachnids.

</p>

<p>

  It is also a reminder that amber fossils can still reveal genuinely new information rather than simply confirming what researchers already knew.

</p>
